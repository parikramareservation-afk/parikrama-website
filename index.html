Experience Dining Like Never Before

Enjoy an unforgettable culinary journey at India’s first revolving restaurant, where every moment brings a new view of Delhi’s skyline.

  • 360° Panoramic Views – Take in breathtaking sights of Connaught Place, India Gate, and beyond as the restaurant gently revolves.
  • Exquisite Multi-Cuisine Menu – Savor a wide selection of Indian, Mughlai, Chinese, and Continental dishes prepared by expert chefs.
  • Unmatched Ambiance – Perfect for romantic dinners, family gatherings, or corporate evenings, offering both luxury and comfort.
  • Iconic Location – Situated in the heart of Delhi, Parikrama combines fine dining with a world-class landmark experience.

Enjoy 360° revolving views of Delhi’s skyline with signature dishes and curated drinks. At Parikrama, every meal becomes a memorable experience.
